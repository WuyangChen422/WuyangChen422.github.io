I am a graduate student pursuing a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence at the City University of Hong Kong, with an expected graduation in **October 2026**. My primary research interests lie in **Embodied AI** and **Autonomous Driving**, and I am actively preparing to apply for collaborative PhD programs for the 2027 academic year.

Previously, I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Technology at Beijing Jiaotong University. 

My current research and development focus includes the path planning and motion control of **Autonomous Underwater Robots (AUR)**, Involving Receding-Horizon Planning, nonlinear ESO, ocean dynamics, as well as simulations based on MuJoCo and ROS. I also have practical engineering experience, including an intership regards generative AI model at Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Science and a front-end development internship at Tencent.

### Research Interests
* Embodied AI
* Autonomous Underwater Robots (AUR) / Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUV)
* World Models
